Introduction

We aim to provide players with a safe, fair and friendly playing environment. By joining FunLand Network you agree to follow all rules listed here. Please read this page in full and use the report / appeal buttons if needed.

Note: All global rules & punishments also apply to Discord. Mutable offences may result in bans if committed outside chat.
